Discovering Hidden Japan
Sarah Johnson

Published: May 15, 2023

4.5 (24 reviews)

When most people think of Japan, they imagine the neon lights of Tokyo or the ancient temples of Kyoto. But Japan has so much more to offer beyond these well-trodden paths. On my recent trip, I set out to discover the hidden gems that make Japan truly special.

The Art Islands of Naoshima

My journey began on the tiny island of Naoshima in the Seto Inland Sea. This remote island has been transformed into an open-air contemporary art museum, with installations and museums designed by world-renowned architects like Tadao Ando.

The Thatched Roof Villages of Shirakawa-go

Next, I traveled to Shirakawa-go, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its traditional gassho-zukuri farmhouses. These houses, with their steep thatched roofs designed to withstand heavy snow, look like something from a fairy tale.

Tips for Off-the-Beaten-Path Japan
  • Learn basic Japanese phrases - English is less common in rural areas
  • Invest in a Japan Rail Pass if traveling extensively
  • Try local specialties in each region
  • Respect local customs, especially in rural areas

Wellness Retreats in Bali
David Chen

Published: April 5, 2023

4.0 (18 reviews)

Bali has long been known as a paradise for those seeking relaxation and rejuvenation. After spending a month exploring the island's wellness offerings, I've compiled this guide to help you find the perfect retreat for your needs.

Fivelements Retreat

Nestled along the Ayung River, Fivelements offers a luxurious Balinese healing experience. Their signature treatments combine traditional Balinese healing with modern wellness practices.

Yoga Barn in Ubud

For yoga enthusiasts, the Yoga Barn is a must-visit. With over 15 daily classes ranging from gentle flow to advanced ashtanga, there's something for every level.

Choosing the Right Retreat
  • Consider your goals (relaxation, detox, spiritual growth)
  • Look at the instructor credentials
  • Check what's included in the package
  • Read recent reviews
